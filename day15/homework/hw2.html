Lion

The lion is known as the king of the jungle. It is a large carnivorous feline with a majestic mane.

Elephant

Elephants are the largest land animals on Earth. They are known for their intelligence and strong social bonds.

Tiger

Tigers are powerful predators with distinctive orange coats and black stripes. They are solitary animals.

Giraffe

Giraffes are the tallest land animals, known for their long necks and legs. They primarily feed on leaves from tall trees.

Zebra

Zebras are known for their distinctive black and white stripes. They are social animals that live in herds.
